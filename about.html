Sean Tobe

is based in sunny San Diego. Along a 23 year journey, Sean has discovered personal passions in audiovisual computer mediums— presently real-time 3-D rendering, data visualizations, and audiovisual installations, from a foundation in videography and photography, post-production, live DJing, and experimental VJing.

New Media Artist, Creative Developer

exploring new forms and impacts of emergent technologies in the arts and culture— creating interactive multimedia experiences from procedural design that aim to inspire reflection on the genuine human connections in virtual communities.
