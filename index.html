Personal statement

Sean Tobe is based in sunny San Diego, earning a Bachelor's Degree at the University of California, San Diego. Along a 22 year journey, Sean has discovered personal passions through audiovisual mediums. Grounded in programming software and maths for Computer Science, the complexity of the underlying technology and its possibilities for humanity has been inspiration to continulously iterate and innovate.
